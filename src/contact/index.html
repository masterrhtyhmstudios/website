FAQs

Where do I park?

Parking Option 1: Castle Car Park (bottom of Crowell Rd). Open until 7pm - park on level 3 of multi-storey car park close to the entrance bridge to the Templars Square Shopping Centre. Walk across the bridge through the blue doors, down first set of steps & turn left through white doors. Master Rhythm Studios is on the right.

Parking Option 2: John Allen Centre Car Park. Parking for 3hrs for free. Once parked, walk across the road and enter the Templars Square Shopping Centre, go up the escalators and continue straight on. Ascend stairs next to Opticians & turn right through white doors. Master Rhythm Studios is on the right.

How do I get access to Master Rhythm Studios after 6pm?

The shopping centre itself closes at 6pm, but Master Rhythm Studios remains open until 10pm. After 6pm use the entrance off Hockmore Street, which is displayed on our website link as Management Suite. On Hockmore Street, the single blue entrance door on the right will be open. Once inside the shopping centre, go up the stairs next to Opticians & turn right through white doors. Master Rhythm Studios is on the right.
