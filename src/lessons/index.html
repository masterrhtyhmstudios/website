AUGUST 2016… SUMMER HOLIDAYS DRUMMING WORKSHOPS AT MASTER RHYTHM STUDIOS!!

The summer workshops we have planned are a great way for kids to spend their free time in the school holidays, developing their musical skills & making new friends! The course will run over two weeks of the summer holidays during day times 9am-3pm & evening times 6:30pm-8:30pm, and will include 10 spaces per session, in Templars Square Cowley, Oxford.

The session structure will include:

  • ‘Playalong’ jams with our in-house PA System!
  • A choice of 3 x Professional Drumkits to play in the main workshop space
  • Use of individual snare practice pads for group sessions
  • Access to drumming booths with Pro Drumkits
  • Refreshments
  • Experienced DBS & safeguard trained drum teachers

Children within the 5-16 age range can attend. Here are the dates:
