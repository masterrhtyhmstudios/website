Summer Holidays Drumming Workshops announced for August 2016!

The Summer Workshops we have planned are a great way for kids to spend their free time in the school holidays, developing their musical skills & making new friends!

The workshops will run over 3 weeks, with a choice of daytime (09:00-15:00) or evening (18:30-20:30) sessions & are held at Master Rhythm Studios in Templars Square, Oxford.

The session structure will include:

  • ‘Playalong’ jams with our in-house PA System!
  • A choice of 3 x Professional Drumkits to play in the main workshop space
  • Use of individual snare practice pads for group sessions
  • Access to drumming booths with Pro Drumkits
  • Refreshments
  • Experienced DBS & Safeguard Trained Drum Teachers

Children in the 5-16 age range can attend.

Dates:

WEEK 1: Monday 1st - Friday 5th August, 2016

WEEK 2: Monday 15th - Friday 19th August, 2016

WEEK 3: Monday 22nd - Friday 26th August, 2016

Prices:

1x session:

  • Daytime Workshop (9am-3pm): £50
  • Evening Workshop: £30
