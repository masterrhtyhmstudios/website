    Tobias Sturmer

    Tobias Sturmer has been a drummer and percussionist for 25 years in the field of Jazz, Latin Jazz and West-African music. He has recently moved to Oxford after living in London for 17 years. There he taught rhythm, drumming, percussion and musicianship at i.a. the City Literary Institute, the Royal Opera House, the Goethe Institute, the Royal College of Music and the Laban Centre. He has performed with many London based bands and African dance companies and has worked extensively in contemporary dance and theatre.

    He holds a MA in Ethnomusicology from SOAS London University. His musical studies have taken him to New York, Dominican Republic, Senegal and Venezuela where he has lived, learned and performed with local musicians.

    Lesson types:
  • Latin and African Percussion
  • Adult rhythm workshops
