Band Rehearsal

The room comes fully equipped with PA System (including Audix mics) & 4 way monitor mix. Also included is a 5 piece Yamaha Maple Custom Drumkit & FREE BACKLINE! We are also offering FREE 5 REHEARSALS FOR THE PRICE OF 4 FOR JUNE & JULY 2016!!
